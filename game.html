Games and Sports

Sports is an integral part of our school's curriculum. Achievements in the field of sports eclipse all other accomplishments because of the sheer excitement that sporting activities generate.

Games and sports play vital role in construction of healthy body and mind.Besides this, sports inculcate a feeling of camaraderie, team spirit, sportsmanship and a sense of responsibility and accountability.. In our school facilities for games and sports like Cricket, Foot ball, Badminton, Kabaddy, Chess, Carom, Throw-ball,and kho-kho etc. have been made available along with the regular exercise. We organize the annual games and sports competition and celebrate the annual prize distribution ceremony to encourage the successful students in it. The school team for different games is constituted under the supervision of the specialist games teachers, which participates in the district and state level games and sports-tournaments.
